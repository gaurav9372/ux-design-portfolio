User Experience

Three stakeholders. One experience. That's the job.

Client
Critic
Users
Judge
Company
Challenger

The trick isn't satisfying any one of them. It's finding the one design that survives all three and still feels obvious. Early-stage work, for me, is mostly that question: where do the user's path, the business goal, and the build constraint line up, and where do they fight? The fights are where the design actually lives.
