How I Work

The same process, every time.

I don't believe in skipping steps. Good design comes from understanding the problem before touching a frame. Here's what every project goes through.

01

Research & Discovery

User interviews, contextual inquiry, heuristic evaluation, and competitive audit. I go broad before going deep — the brief is never the full picture.

02

Define & Strategize

Personas, journey maps, and problem framing. I synthesise research into a clear design brief before wireframing a single screen.

03

Design & Iterate

Low-fi to high-fi, tested early and often. I run usability tests during wireframing — not after hi-fi is done.

04

Deliver & Measure

Handoff-ready design systems, annotated specs, and post-launch review. The work isn't done until the metrics prove it.
