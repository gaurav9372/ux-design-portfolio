About me

I design digital products that make complex things feel simple.

Based in Varanasi, India. Currently open to freelance and full-time opportunities.

My Story

I got into design the way most people do. By accident.

Today I design across web and mobile, mostly for startups and small product teams.

What makes me different is probably my process.

What I Believe

01

Research before rectangles

I do not open Figma until I understand who I am designing for and why.

02

Constraints are direction

Tight deadlines and technical limits are not problems.

03

Ship and learn

A good design shipped today beats a perfect design shipped never.

04

Developers are design partners

My best work happens when I sit with the people who build what I design.
