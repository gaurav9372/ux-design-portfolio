Things people usually ask

What kind of projects do you take on?

I work on web and mobile product design, mostly with startups and small teams. E-commerce, SaaS, healthcare, fintech. If it involves designing for real users, I am interested.

What is your typical timeline?

Most projects take 4 to 12 weeks depending on scope. I can usually give you a rough estimate after our first conversation about the project.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. I have worked with teams across different time zones. I am flexible with scheduling and comfortable with async communication.

How do you usually work?

I start with understanding the problem through research, then move into wireframes and prototypes. I work closely with developers and iterate based on feedback and testing.
